1. Notes

Real estate is all about wasting time. The more time you waste, the more money you can take. (p. 4)

Always develop on the area growth is comming. If you are a little early, the growth will bail you out. If you develop on an area that the growth is already done or it not continuing, if you are wrong, you are wrong forever. (p. 18)

Auction helps both seller and buyer. When market hot, seller can sell with higher price, when the market is slow, seller can sell their property faster, then they can use the money to reinvest instead of morgage to reinvest. Buyer can buy with minimum price. (p. 27)

Invest in single-family rental house is easy to sell, manage and profit. (p. 29)

In 2006, accoding to Forbes. There were 800 billionaries in the world, more than 60 billionaries make their wealth in real estate. (p. 41)

The first offer is usually the best. Quick buyer highly properly the person need to buy. (p. 56)

Good partner (broker) is more important than good value of real estate. (p. 61)

Dont follow crowd. Like any other investment, it comes down to risk versus reward. (p. 68)

Real estate is not relationship business. Work with who identity can help you archive your goal instead of people you like. work with people you like is just a bonus.
It is nice to have the best location, but in the world today, the key is specilization. (p. 80)

Like many simple yet sage words, it took 5 mins to learn and a lifetime to master. (p. 96)

The real profit is made when real estate is bought not when it is sold. (p. 100)

Do not force to sell real estate at specific time. (p. 101)

There is no bad time to buy real estate as long as we have right strategy. (p. 108)

When amateurs get in, it is time to get out. (p. 120)

Do not put your emotion in real estate. (p. 140)

Buy home and invest money to real estate are different. Buy home with heart, invest real estate with head. (p. 147)

“No” really mean “convince me”. Sellers can sell any products as long as they can give buyer right reason to buy it. (p. 165)

Dont wait to buy real estate. Buy real estate and wait. (p. 174)

Close the deal before holiday, weekends. If you can’t close the deal before holiday weekends, 50% chance you will lost the deal. Your customer will have time with their family and they are easy to change their mind. (p. 219)

Doing business is like playing chess, it is all about strategy, plans, experience, knowledge. (p. 228)

In order to buy real estate in unfamiliar market. It is better if we ask colleagues or friends live their instead of trying myself to master it. (p. 243)

Buy real estate when other people think it is our of favor. (p. 261)

When you buy home, “buy neighborhood, not house”. (p. 267)
