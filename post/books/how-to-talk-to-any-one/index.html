1. Notes

How to win friends and influence people is old one, for 21st century, we need new techneque.

Smile slowly, the way we smile is very important.

Big winners have 50 vocabularies more than others, try to use as many as vocabulary as possible

Try to get quoted from books, internet, use them when you get in the talk show.

Never ever make a joke at anyone elses expense. You’ll wind up paying for it, dearly.

When give good/bad new to someone, sympathetic to them.

How to response the question which we don’t want to answer? Kindly answer simply, then if interrogator repeat the question, still answer the same words and same tone & voice.

How to talk to celebrity

  • Do not take their time, dont slobber over them.
  • If they grance us, smile back to them.
  • If we are in the conference or restaurant with them, wait until they get out and come & talk to them, talk very shortly. How they help you?

Don’t say thank you alone, have to say thank you + something. Ex: Thank you for your help, thank you for your attendant.

“Try the new thing, try to play everything, at least one time. It will help us experience with a lot of thing, it will help the conversation in the future with stranger much better. Ex: Go to hiking, play tennis, go to art gallery. It will help us become insider. In the conversation many people, insiders are people talking, outsiders are people keep quiet”

How to make you alike their family

  • Use their words, listen to their vocabularies because their vocabs usually be different with your vocabs, echo their vocabs

When prise/compliment someone.
Should compiment to very close friend of that one. That close fiend will definetely tell to that person.

When talking with somebody through phone.
Please careful, every expression have to be shown as sound or words because that person don’t see your face, don’t see your expression.

Confident person don’t scare to be looked at. they always want to be at the center of the group.

“Should have a small noted book, take notes specific thing of each people around you. When meet them again, used them to talk to make them feel like they are special.”

Be the first person applause when listen in the group. Don’t wait someone else reponse, be the first person response.
